Apocalypse gives you more!!!

Apocalypse Airsoft is a mixed woodland site of approximately 100 acres, in the heart of Kent between the M2 and M20.
The site features a Vietnamese village and US Military Base, but there is no fixed dress code for skirmishers

On Site Shop

The onsite shop provides a range of hot and cold snacks and supplies including. Ammunition, Pyro, Gas, Field Kit, Weaponry and much more!!
